Health - Recommended inoculations for travel may change and you should consult your doctor for current recommendations before you depart. The World Health Organization and the International Civil Aviation Organization have approved the following disinsection procedures: (1) spray the aircraft cabin with an aerosolized insecticide while passengers are on board or (2) treat the aircraft's interior surfaces with a residual insecticide while passengers are not on board.

8. AIRLINE SCHEDULE CHANGE
Changes to flight schedules (including cancellations, delays and other schedule changes) can occur for any number of reasons, including bad weather, mechanical problems, crew issues and civil unrest, and can happen at any time, including after check-in, whether in person or online. Such schedule changes ("Schedule Changes") can also include the change of your original flight from a direct non-stop itinerary to include a stop(s) and/or connection(s) to your final destination. Please note that all Schedule Changes stem from the airlines directly.

Each airline has its own policy regarding the options, if any, available to you in the event of a Schedule Change, which may differ for different types of Schedule Changes. These options may require action from you in order for them to be available to you including requiring your express consent to confirm a change to your itinerary. Please review the applicable fare rules, to see what options may be available to you in the event of a Schedule Change. Check your fare rules and you can always call us for assistance with your particular ticket.

In order to help our customers remain informed regarding the status of their flights, and to protect our customers' airline tickets from being invalidated, we have implemented the following guidelines for when Schedule Changes, including cancellations, occur, so that we can try to notify our customers of changes to their itinerary:

We will make every effort to notify you of both major and minor Schedule Changes by email, using the contact information you provide during booking. All such notifications will be made as soon as reasonably practicable upon us learning of the Schedule Change from the airline.
The classification of "major" and "minor" Schedule Changes varies widely by airlines. Please check your inbox for any email notification from us regarding such Schedule Changes, since these changes may require you to take additional action.
Minor Schedule Changes

If a Schedule Change is classified as a "minor" Schedule Change by the airline, you will be automatically re-confirmed on an alternate flight, unless the Schedule Change would result in a misconnection. At that time, we will send you notification of the Schedule Change and an updated itinerary.
Should the re-confirmed flight not be suitable, please call our customer service center. We will make every effort to provide you with alternative accommodation, if and as permitted by your airline's fare rules
If the revised itinerary is acceptable, you are not required to take any additional action

Major Schedule Changes

If a Schedule Change is classified as a "major" Schedule Change by the airline or will result in a misconnection, we will send you an email notification of the Schedule Change along with a request to confirm or request modification of the flight re-accommodation offered by your airline.
Please note: This email will contain an airline-imposed deadline by which you must respond in order to protect the validity of your ticket. Should you fail to respond by the deadline, Walabu Travel will automatically confirm the re-accommodation option provided by your carrier in order to avoid cancellation of your ticket by the airline.
Depending on the amount of time in advance in which we learn of a Schedule Change, we will attempt to send additional Schedule Change notifications and/or reminders of actions you are required to take to preserve your ticket
Turkish Airlines Schedule Changes:

We will do our best to find you an alternative flight re-accommodation when the Schedule Change occurs, depending on the airline's available inventory and fare rules. As such, when a Schedule Change occurs with Turkish Airlines and we are not able to find you an alternative flight re-accommodation within the permissible timeframe imposed by Turkish Airlines, we will automatically proceed to refund your original booking minus any non-refundable taxes on the original method of payment used.
Although we make every effort to provide you with Schedule Change information as described above, circumstances outside our control may impede our ability to do so. Accordingly, please note the following:

We will not be responsible if you fail to enter the correct contact information at time of reservation. Please review for accuracy all contact information (e.g. email address, phone number) you provide to us during booking to ensure you receive updates.
If Schedule Changes occur after you have checked in for your flight (including online), your airline has taken control of your ticket, and it is now your airline's responsibility to notify you of the change and to rebook your flight to your final destination.
Sometimes the airline does not provide advance notice of a Schedule Change. For this reason, it is your responsibility to reconfirm your flight directly with us or with the airline 72 hours prior to your scheduled departure, as well as 24 hours prior to your scheduled flight date to safeguard against any potential missed flights.

Potential restrictions and/or limitations include:

Fares eligible to rebook in the future may be limited to the same fare type and/or fare class as your original ticket(s).
Your original ticket price consisted of individual fare and taxes components. In some cases, the fare and taxes components of your original ticket may be respectively limited to the fare and tax portions of your new itinerary.
The original value of your ticket(s) is non-refundable and non-transferable from one traveller to another.
If the cost of your new flight is greater than your original ticket, you will need to pay the remaining difference in price. If the cost of your new flight is less than your original ticket price, you may lose the remaining value depending on the airline policy.
You may be restricted to rebooking a ticket that has the same origin and/or destination as your original ticket.
The booking date, travel commence by date and/or travel completed by date of your trip must be within the validity period allowed by the airline (e.g. 12 or 24 months from the original booking date or travel date, depending on the airline policy).
You must rebook a ticket with the same airline as the original ticket(s).
The value of your original ticket cannot be used for the purchase of ancillary services;
Upon rebooking, you may be charged an airline penalty fee per passenger by the airline.

Your reservation must be cancelled prior to the scheduled departure date or you risk losing the full value of your ticket.

18. SUPER SAVER FARES/VIRTUAL INTERLINE

Voluntary Changes: Each individual ticket is subject to its own rules and restrictions and customers are deemed to be point-to-point ticket holders. For more information, please refer to the Fees section.

Airline initiated Changes: Unlike round-trip tickets, if one of these flights is affected by an airline-initiated change (cancelation or involuntary schedule change) that causes a customer to make changes to the other flights, the customer will be responsible for any fees incurred for making changes to the unaffected flights.

Self-Transfer and Misconnections: By choosing a Super Saver itinerary, you are responsible for the self-transfer between connecting flights. In some cases, the terminal for your next flight could be different from the one you landed at.

You are responsible for all expenses in the purchasing of a new flight ticket should you have to rebook, due to a misconnection.

Check-in: You are responsible to check into each flight separately since there are no interline agreements between the carriers and in some cases to pass through additional security checkpoints.

Visa Requirements: In some cases, you could be leaving the transit zone and have to pass through passport control yet again. You will need to have the proper travel documents per each ticket booked. For more information, please refer to the Visa Section.

Baggage Allowance: Each individual airline has its own baggage allowance policy. Please ensure your luggage size, weight and quantity correspond to the policies of all operating carriers with a Super Saver itinerary.

Baggage Transfer: When booking a Super Saver fare, you are responsible for the "self-transfer" of baggage between the two carriers at the airport in cases where you have separate independent connecting flights.
